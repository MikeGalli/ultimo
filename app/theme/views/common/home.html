WonderWork

We believe beauty is not measured by an age, a dollar sign or stereotype and that we do not need to be fixed, plumped, erased or changed. We believe that beauty is achieved by how you live your life, and the choices you make. One choice we have made is to try to live everyday with gratitude and to share in our abundance. So, we have partnered with WonderWork, an organization committed to saving the lives of children and their families by providing free, life-changing surgeries. For every dollar you spend, we donate 1% to the cause. And we are grateful that we have so many Kari Gran clients that make this gift possible. Whether it’s a lip whip or a system purchase, you are helping to change lives. Now, isn’t that beautiful.
